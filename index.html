Sports lighting FAQ

Do you provide photometric lighting plans?

Yes—our layouts target uniformity, glare control, and spill reduction based on your sport and level of play.

Can you retrofit existing poles and wiring?

In many cases, yes. We evaluate your current infrastructure and recommend the most cost-effective upgrade path.

Do you offer controls and scheduling?

Yes—scene control, scheduling, and remote access are available depending on the system selected.
