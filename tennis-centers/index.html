Tennis Centers

Even, low-glare lighting for competition and training.

Tennis lighting demands consistent illumination with minimal glare—so players can track the ball cleanly from every angle. Texas Sports Lighting designs court layouts that prioritize uniformity, player comfort, and spill control for neighboring courts and properties.

  • Fixture selection & optics
  • Glare shields & spill control
  • Controls & scheduling
