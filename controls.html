Controls

Simple, powerful lighting control

Bring your sports lighting under one easy-to-use platform. From schedules and game-night scenes to remote access and reporting, controls help you get more out of every watt.

We design control solutions around how your facilities actually operate—league play, practices, rentals, tournaments, and off-hours security. Turn lights on from the parking lot, automate recurring events, and give coaches or staff the access they need without handing out breaker keys.

What controls can do for your facility

  • Web & mobile access from any connected device
  • Scenes for games, practice, maintenance, and events
  • Schedules tied to fields, courts, or groups of poles
  • User roles with per-field or per-group permissions
  • Energy usage, runtime logs, and basic reporting
  • Text/email alerts for issues or after-hours usage

See it in action

We can walk you through a typical control screen—showing how to schedule fields, trigger scenes, and check status from your phone or desktop.

  • Live or virtual walkthrough
  • Sample facility layouts
  • Q&A on integrations and phasing

Built for real-world operations

Every facility team is different. These core capabilities help you run fields and courts smoothly, while keeping things simple for day-to-day users.

Schedules & Access

Keep lights tied to your calendar instead of the breaker panel. Give coaches access to only what they need, when they need it.

  • Recurring schedules by field or court
  • One-time events and tournaments
  • Role-based access for staff and partners

Scenes & Presets

Create repeatable lighting looks for different uses—full game, practice, half-field, or maintenance lighting with a single tap.

  • Custom presets per sport or level
  • Reduced-output practice modes
  • Simple triggers for staff and coaches

Monitoring & Reporting

See which fields are on, how long they’ve been running, and where you’re saving energy compared to older systems.

  • Runtime history by field or group
  • Basic energy and usage summaries
  • Notifications for unexpected runtime

Integration & Growth

Start with a single field or complex and expand over time. Tie controls into future systems as your facility grows.

  • Roadmap for phased rollouts
  • Support for multi-field complexes
  • Options for API/BMS integration
