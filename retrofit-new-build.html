Retrofits & New Build

Whether you’re upgrading an existing field or planning a new facility from the ground up, we help you choose the right path for performance, budget, and long-term efficiency.

For retrofits, we evaluate your existing poles, wiring, and power to see how far we can take your current infrastructure with modern LED fixtures and controls. For new builds, we work alongside your design team to create a complete sports lighting package that’s ready for installation and future expansion.

Our process

  1. Site audit & discussion of goals
  2. Photometrics, fixture layout, and recommendations
  3. Controls, scheduling, and energy strategy
  4. Installation coordination & commissioning support

How we support your project

Every facility is different. These core services help match the right fixtures, optics, and controls to your level of play and budget.

LED Retrofit Upgrades

Replace outdated HID or metal halide fixtures with efficient LED systems using as much of your existing infrastructure as possible.

  • Improved light levels & uniformity
  • Reduced energy and maintenance
  • Targeted optics to cut spill & glare

New Build Design

Ground-up lighting layouts for new fields, courts, and stadiums, coordinated with architects and engineers.

  • Complete photometric layouts
  • Pole locations & aiming plans
  • Future-ready for added fields or levels

Poles & Structures

Evaluate existing poles or design new structures to support modern LED weight, EPA, and wind-load requirements.

  • Existing pole assessment
  • Mounting & crossarm options
  • Recommendations for replacements

Controls & Phasing

Layer in smart controls now or later, with flexible phasing to match budgets and construction schedules.

  • Remote scheduling & scenes
  • Energy reporting & monitoring
  • Stage work across multiple seasons
